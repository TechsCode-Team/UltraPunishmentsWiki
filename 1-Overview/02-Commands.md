# Commands
Here is a list of all the commands that can be used.
<br>

* `/upun`
  To open the Punishment GUI
* `/ban <User> <Server> <Time> [Reason]`
  To ban a player
* `/unban <User>`
  To unban a player
* `/mute <User> <Time> [Reason]`
  To mute a player
* `/unmute <User>`
  To unmute a player
* `/freeze <User> <Time> [Reason]`
  To freeze a player
* `/unfreeze <User>`
  To unfreeze a player
* `/kick <User> [Reason]`
  To kick a player
* `/warn <User> <Server> <Kick|Message> [Reason]`
  To warn a player
* `/clearchat`
  Clears the entire chat for everyone
* `/mutechat`
  To mute the chat for normal players
<br>

## Symbols:
 - <> = Required
 - [] = Optional
